About Us and You

🌍
We believe recycling can save the world — one item at a time.

RecycleHelper was created by people who care deeply about the environment. Our goal is simple: make recycling easy, accessible, and even fun for everyone. Whether you're a first-time recycler or a seasoned eco-warrior, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

What We Do
📚
Educate

We break down complex recycling rules into clear, easy-to-understand guides. Learn exactly which bin to use, what gets recycled, and why it matters.

🎮
Engage

Our interactive game "Save the City" lets you experience the real impact of recycling decisions — in a fun, hands-on way that sticks with you.

🤝
Empower

We give you the tools and knowledge to make a real difference in your community. Every informed choice you make ripples outward to inspire others.
